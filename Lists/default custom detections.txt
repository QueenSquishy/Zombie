
The below items are concepts that everyone should look to apply as custom detections whenever you deploy an EDR tool. Take consideration for criticality and your own triage flows.

	1. Tampering with sensors or other security products. 
	2. Scripts inside temporary directories.
	3. Known bad drivers (https://github.com/eclypsium/Screwed-Drivers/blob/master/DRIVERS.md).
	4. LOLBAS rename attempts (https://lolbas-project.github.io/)
	5. Windows Firewall Disabled
	6. Copying of browser data stores
	7. Disabling of UAC
	8. WMI Executing files
	9. Shadow Volumes being deleted
	10. Device being set to safemode using bcdedit cmdline
